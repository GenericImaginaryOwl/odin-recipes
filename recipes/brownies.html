Brownies

Photo by Bethany Hicks.

Description

  • Dark chocolate (55% cacao and up), 200g.
  • Butter, 150g.
  • 3 eggs.
  • Sugar, 90-180g (for desired sweetness).
  • Flour, 70g.
  • Cacao powder 30-40g.

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 170C.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in a water bath.
  3. Whisk sugar and eggs, then mix in molten butter with chocolate.
  4. Mix flour and cacao powder in a separate bowl.
  5. Add the resulting mixture into chocolate one.
  6. Pour the batter into a baking tray and put it in the oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Cut after it's all cooled down.
