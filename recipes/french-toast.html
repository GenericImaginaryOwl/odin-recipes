French Toast

Photo by Andrew Currie, imported from Wikimedia.

Description

Makes for a great breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3-5 eggs.
  • Salt.
  • Sliced bread.
  • Vegetable oil.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. (Optional) Toast the sliced bread.
  2. Whisk eggs and salt in a bowl untill it looks uniform.
  3. Dip slices of bread in the mixture.
  4. Preheat the pan with a bit of vegetable oil on it (you can also add a small slice of butter).
  5. Put Slices of bread on a pan and cover and put a lid on it.
  6. Flip the pieces once the bottom side of the toasts looks golden.
  7. After the same happens to the other side, the dish is ready to serve (putting a piece of buttrt on top is an option).
