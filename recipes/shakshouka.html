Shakshouka

Photo by Polina Tankilevitch.

Description

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs.
  • 2 table spoons of milk.
  • Butter.
  • 1 tomato (you can use crushed, for convenience).
  • Any cheese.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Grate a quarter of a bowl of cheese.
  3. Cut tomato into thin slices and put those on a hot pan with a piece butter.
  4. After all the butter dissolves and tomato slices soften up, pour egg mixture onto the pan.
  5. Add grated cheese and mix with spatula untill eggs solidify.
  6. Ready to serve.
